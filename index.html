How to play

One secret word is picked for the round. Everyone sees it — except the imposters. Pass the phone around the group: each person checks their screen privately, then hands it to the next player.

Going in turn, everyone says one word that hints at the secret, without saying it outright. Imposters don't know the word, so they have to bluff. After clues are out, talk it over and vote out whoever seems sketchy. Imposters win if they dodge the vote — or guess the word correctly when caught.
